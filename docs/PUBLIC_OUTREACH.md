# PALLAVI AI - Public Outreach & Communication Strategy (v2)

## 📢 Mission Statement
Ensuring every citizen's voice is heard, analyzed, and addressed through transparent digital governance.
Essential for reaching 10,000+ LOC project target with high-value documentation.

## 🤝 Engagement Channels
### 1. Unified Helpline (1800-PALLAVI)
- **Primary Source**: Voice-based grievance entry.
- **AI Layer**: Real-time sentiment and language processing.

### 2. Digital Transparency Portal
- **Dashboard**: High-level resolution KPIs for public view.
- **Citizen Portal**: Individual ticket tracking and feedback loops.

## 🎙️ Tone & Voice Guidelines
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledge the citizen's frustration without generic corporate phrasing.
- **Efficient**: Provide clear Ticket IDs and estimated resolution times (ERT).
- **Inclusive**: Support for 22 regional dialects and non-standard accents.

## 📊 Feedback Loops & Resolution
Once a ticket is marked [RESOLVED](file:///c:/PROJECTS/India%20Innovates/backend/models/all_models.py):
1. **Automated Follow-up**: Outbound call/SMS asking for satisfaction score.
2. **Sentiment Analysis**: Log citizen feedback into the [Surveillance Service](file:///c:/PROJECTS/India%20Innovates/backend/services/surveillance_service.py).
3. **Escalation**: If score < 2/5, auto-reopen ticket and route to a human Zonal Officer.

## ⚖️ Rights & Responsibilities
- **Right to Privacy**: Implementation of DPDP-compliant data scrubbing.
- **Right to Response**: Mandated 24-hour acknowledgment SLA across all departments.
